Perspectives on the World Christian Movement in Austin

Perspectives is a 15-week course that will change the way you view the world around you. You'll have your eyes opened to the heart and purpose of God and see how you can take part in His work all over the world. A fantastic array of international missiologists and instructors will challenge and inspire you with their personal experiences that will open your eyes to see just how big God is and how much He desires that all might come to know Him.

You can read more about the class on the Perspectives website.
